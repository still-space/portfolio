About

Hi, I'm Daria

I'm a Senior Product Designer based in Warsaw, with over 10 years of experience across iGaming, SaaS, logistics, and real estate tech. I've worked with teams on three continents, designing products that serve millions of users in markets from Latin America to Southeast Asia.

What sets me apart is the intersection of craft and systems thinking. I don't just design screens — I build scalable design systems, run UX research to validate decisions, and use product analytics to measure impact. Right now I'm deepening my analytics skills at Projector Institute (Product Analytics & Data Mining program).

I believe the best design happens when you deeply understand both the user's pain and the business constraint. That's where I thrive — turning that tension into something clear, useful, and beautiful.

When I'm not designing, I'm traveling, hiking, or training my Lagotto Romagnolo puppy. My curiosity about psychology and human behavior shapes everything I design.
