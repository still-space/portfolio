Context

The platform is a global betting product operating across Africa, Asia, and LATAM. It combines sports betting, live betting, and casino in a single product. When I joined, the platform suffered from fragmented UX, outdated visuals, and heavy developer dependency for any content changes.

Challenges

  • Fragmented experience across sports, live, and casino sections — each felt like a separate product
  • Outdated visuals creating low trust, especially in competitive markets
  • Weak localization — one-size-fits-all approach for vastly different markets
  • High friction in the betslip and deposit flows leading to significant drop-offs
  • Regional operators relied on developers for every page change

After

My Approach

I started by mapping the entire user journey across all three verticals (sports, live, casino) to identify where the experience broke down. The biggest conversion leak was in the deposit flow — a 6-step onboarding funnel that lost nearly half of new users.

Key design decisions

  • Unified navigation: Merged sports, live, and casino into a single coherent product with consistent patterns, shared components, and smooth transitions between sections
  • Deposit flow simplification: Reduced from 6 steps to 3, with local payment methods pre-selected by region
  • Multi-theme design system: Built a token-based system in Figma with 200+ components, supporting multiple brand themes and RTL layouts for future market expansion
  • Widget-based homepage: Regional operators can build pages from flexible widgets (see SDUI Builder case study)
  • Localized payments: Region-specific payment UX adapted to local habits and regulations

Design System

A token-based, multi-theme design system with 200+ components built in Figma. Supports light/dark themes, RTL layouts, and multiple brand skins — enabling 3 product teams to ship consistently across 5 markets.

Color Tokens

Semantic color system organized by function (background, control, text, icon) — ensuring consistent theming across dark, light, and brand variations.
