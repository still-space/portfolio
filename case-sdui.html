SDUI Page Builder

A visual builder that empowers regional operators to manage gambling platform interfaces independently — fast, intuitive, and without developer involvement.

Company
Growe
Role
Lead Product Designer
Year
2024–2025
Platform
Web (Internal Tool)

Context

Growe operates iGaming brands across Africa, Asia, and Latin America. Each region has unique regulatory requirements, player preferences, and promotional calendars. Regional operators needed to update platform content constantly — new promotions, seasonal layouts, localized widgets.

The problem: every interface change required a developer. A simple homepage update could take 2–3 weeks from request to deployment.

Discovery

I interviewed 6 regional operators and 4 product managers to map the end-to-end content update workflow. The bottleneck was clear: operators knew exactly what they wanted but had no way to execute without engineering support.

Key pain points

  • 2–3 week turnaround for any page or layout change
  • Operators couldn't run A/B tests on page layouts independently
  • Different regions needed different widget configurations, but the platform was one-size-fits-all
  • Promotional windows were often missed because of deployment delays

Design Solution

I designed SDUI Builder — a visual interface where operators can configure pages by dragging and dropping pre-built widgets, editing content inline, and publishing changes instantly.

Core capabilities

  • Layout management: Configure pages per channel (Mobile, Desktop, Android, iOS) and per market
  • Widget library: Sports listings, casino carousels, promotions, jackpots, live streams, leaderboards, and more
  • Drag & drop: Rearrange widgets, add or remove sections visually
  • Inline editing: Update widget content directly in the builder preview
  • Segmentation: Tailor layouts to specific player segments (VIPs vs. newcomers)
  • Change history: Track all updates with full audit trail for compliance
  • Layout cloning: Duplicate pages across markets, then localize
